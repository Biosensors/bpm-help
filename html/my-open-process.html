My Open Process

Understanding how to track my open processes

  • Overview
  • View My Open Process
  • Send Reminder

Overview

The My Open Processes function allow you to track your submitted processes which are still pending action and also manually trigger reminder to them.

View My Open Process

  1. Click on "My Open Processes" menu link
  2. Your open processes (if any) will be shown in the listing
    • close, cancel or draft processes will not be shown here. You may locate these processes through search process function.
  3. Click on "View Detail" link to view the processing status

Send Reminder

  1. System by default will show pending step in "Amber" color tab
    • You may toggle to other tab to view other steps processing status
  2. For processing status, system will show who has approved / who is pending action and also info for reminder
  3. if needed, you may manually trigger reminder by clicking on "Send Emaiil Reminder" button.
