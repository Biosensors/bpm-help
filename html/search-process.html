Overview

The Search Processes function allow you search all the processes that you have submitted, involved in action or granted with view access.

Search Process

  1. Click on "Search Processes" menu link
  2. Specify search criteria

  3. Click on "Search" button to perform search
  4. From display result, click on "Take Action"
  5. Click on "My Open Process" to view the process details and processing status
    • refer to "My Open Processes" section for more details
    • Action will not be shown if process is closed/rejected
  6. Click on "Process Step View" to view the process in a step by step view
    • refer to "View Process Step" section for screenshot
  7. Click on "Download Report" to download process detail to PDF
    • All process revision will be available in the PDF report

  8. Click on "View Template" to view the template configuration.
