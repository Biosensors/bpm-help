Overview

The Search Processes function allow you search all the processes that you have submitted, involved in action or granted with view access.

Search Process

  1. Click on "Search Processes" menu link
  2. Enter search criteria
  3. Click on "Search" button to perform search
  4. From display result, click on "Take Action"
  5. Click on "My Open Process" to view the process details and processing status
    • refer to "My Open Processes" section for more details
  6. Click on "Process Step View" to view the process in a step by step view
    • refer to "View Process Step" section for screenshot
  7. Click on "Grant Access" if you want to grant other who is not involved in the process to be able to access the process
    • refer to "Grant Access" section for screenshot

View Process Step

Grant Access

Process by default is viewable for all the people involved in the process and also to thier reporting line traverse up to senior management. However, if you like others who is not involved to view process, you may grant the access by following below steps.
  1. Search for the user you want to grant access
  2. Provide remarks on why access need to be granted
  3. Click on "Submit" button
    • Email will be triggered to the user to inform access has been granted
