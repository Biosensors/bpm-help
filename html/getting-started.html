Overview

BPM is a software tool designed to help bring paper process online and streamline business processes for optimal efficiency. This tool allows you to create form and setting a sequential process path for the process to follow until it is fully processed. Key Features


Login

No account creation needed as BPM is integrated with Active Directory. Just login using your windows ID and password and you can start to build template or raise process !

Note : The BPM is operate under company network. Please use VPN if you need to access from public.

  1. Click on the login button
  2. Enter your windows user ID (e.g. ms.tan)
  3. Enter your windows login password
  4. Select your company domain
  5. Click on "Submit" button
  1. You will see your name display upon successful login.
  2. To logout, simply click on your name, follow by anotger click on the "Logout" button in the profile dialog .


Mobile Friendly

Dont forget also that BPMS is mobile friendly system and it streamlined experience across different devices. You could access and use the system either through your computer or mobile devices (i.e iOS or Android devices).
